How The System Works

From visitor to booked appointment - automatically

1

Visitor lands on your focused page

They come from Google Ads, Facebook, or local search. The page is designed with one goal: get them to inquire.

2

AI chat engages & qualifies

The chat widget pops up, answers their questions, and collects their contact information if they're interested.

3

Lead is captured & notified

The lead automatically goes to a Google Sheet or your CRM. You get an instant text/email notification.

4

Automated follow-up begins

The system sends an immediate response to the lead. If they don't reply, it sends reminders according to your schedule.

5

You call with a prepared script

You call the lead with a simple script that converts at a higher rate. The system tracks everything.

6

Monthly optimization

We review what's working, adjust the ads and page, and optimize to get you more booked appointments.
